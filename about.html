Amy Wang is an high school-aged artist based in Charlottesville, Virginia.

Born in California and raised in Virginia, Amy takes inspiration from the vast amount of nature around her and the destinations she has traveled to.

Being a digital and traditional artist, Amy works with various mediums and the app Procreate. Her favorite medium is oil pastel, and her close second is charcoal.
