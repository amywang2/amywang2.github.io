Amy Wang is an artist based in Charlottesville, Virginia.

Amy Wang is a high school-aged artist and aspiring engineer born in Redwood City, California. She is a digital and traditional artist, working with various mediums and the app Procreate. Her favorite medium is oil pastel, and her close second is charcoal.

Growing up in Virginia, she takes inspiration from the vast amounts of nature around her and the destinations she has traveled to.
